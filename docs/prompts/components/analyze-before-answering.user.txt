ANALYZE BEFORE ANSWERING

Purpose
- Add a short analysis pass before the final answer.
- Does not define reading coverage, evidence policy, or output format.

Rules
- Do not answer immediately after the first pass.
- First identify the main evidence, constraints, conflicts, and uncertainties in the provided material.
- Then produce the final answer under the active workflow and evidence policy.
- Do not invent missing facts, context, or conclusions during the analysis pass.
